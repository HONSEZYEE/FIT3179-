Global YouTube Subscribers by Country

Bubble size represents total subscribers aggregated by country.

Mean Subscribers by Category (Country Select)

Compare content categories by average subscribers.

Proportion of Brand Channel vs Non-Brand Channel

Share of channels identified as brand vs non-brand.

By analyzing the left chart, which compares the average number of subscribers across different content categories in the selected country, we can identify which types of content are more popular there. The right chart illustrates the structure of channel types. Together, they reveal not only “what kind of content is trending”, but also whether those popular categories are primarily commercial or non-commercial in nature.
